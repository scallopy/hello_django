"Elektra - Dimitar Dimitrov" company started its activity in 1990. He works in the field of electronics - autoelectronics and all types of GPS systems, video surveillance and security systems.

The company offers its customers fast and quality service based on years of experience, selected staff of qualified specialists, modern equipment for high-tech services and innovative diagnostic equipment.

Autoelectronics "Elektra" is a service-trade complex with about 1200 sq.m of working space in Bulgaria, the city of Rousse, 68, Gotse Delchev Blvd.

For customer convenience, the company also has an autoelectronics service in Bulgaria, the city of Rousse, on 17, Vidin Street.

At the same address, the store offers a wide range of xenon lights and elements for their installation, imported by "PSP Computers" Ltd - the official importer of LUXIMA products in Bulgaria. “Electra - Dimitar Dimitrov” is an authorized workshop for their installation.

Autoelectronics Electra offers the following services for vehicles:

  • Computer diagnostics of electrical control blocks (clearing of accumulated errors)
  • KTC Diagnostic and Repair of Gasoline and Diesel Engines
  • Repair of starters, alternators and electrical equipment
  • Computer Diagnostics and Repair of Petrol and Diesel Injection Systems ABS, SRS, etc.
  • Periodic reviews
  • Repair electronics of the vehicle
  • Repairs to the car's electrical system
  • Maintenance and repair of the air conditioning system
  • Sale and installation of Car audio systems, CD, DVD, TV, Multimedia
  • Installation of Professional Audio Systems in vehicles
  • Sale, installation, and updating of the vehicle's navigation system
  • Installation of GPS tracking systems
  • Installation of alarm systems
  • Installation and repair of all types of immobilizers, Bluetooth, APS car systems
  • Repair of computers, modules and electrical circuit boards of vehicles
  • Repair and installation of central locking of vehicles
  • Repair of electric stoves on vans, trucks, and buses
  • Installation of Xenon systems
  • Repair of electric vehicles
  • Mechanical repairs
  • Changing oils, filters, etc.
